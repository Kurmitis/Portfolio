Parkour

I started doing parkour in 2016. Back then I used to be a small, fat child. At first training was really difficult, but as time passed, I started making lots of progress and in 2018 I could already do flips outside.

Parkour helped me overcome many mental and physical barriers, it helped me develop as a person, that's why i am so happy that I started doing it, as it really changed my life.

Parkour also sparked my interest in action photography as well as video filming & editing.
